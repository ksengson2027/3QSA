HOW TO PLAY BALINGKICLAW


How well do you know the badingkalats?




  1. Insert a coin to start.
  2. You will be given 7 attempts to get the plushies.
  3. After a successful catch, you will have to guess who that plushie represents in Balingkilat.
  4. For each miss and wrong guess, you will lose 1 try.
  5. To be able to receive your caught plushies, you will need 5 successful attempts.

Note: You will receive a special plushie if you successfully attain your 5 plushies. Press “You Win“ to play the game again.





TIPS AND TRICKS






Claw Machine

Be precise with timing

Make sure your claw is centered to your target plushie

Guessing the Balingkilat Student

Notice and remember the redeeming quality of the plushie that you picked.
